Taiwan Startup Terrace

As part of efforts to transform Taiwan into a leading innovation service provider in the Asia-Pacific region, the SMEA, MOEA has established a major international startup hub, namely Startup Terrace, at the site of the former Universiade athlete’s village in Linkou, New Taipei City. Startup Terrace provides offices, housing, co-working space, an exhibition center, and other state-of-the-art facilities that make it the ideal place for startups to grow and develop.

Asia New Bay Area in Kaohsiung

Chunghwa Telecom and 13 vendors have officially stationed in Asia New Bay Area in Kaohsiung to connect 5G industries in Taiwan to the world. Initial resources include fiber and wireless networks, cloud computing rooms, edge cloud computing platforms, and 5G enterprise networks. This region will be proof-of-concept area for 5G vertical applications, including smart manufacturing, AR/VR application, drone inspection and logistics, smart streetlights, and environmental detection. It can work with local and international companies and startups to verify feasibility, and expand to commercial scale for industrial use. Top-tier teams in Taiwan will also export 5G AIoT solutions to the world, and supply global demands.
